## Categories & Content

### Lyrics Deep Dive (new category)
Analyse the lyrics of a song and describe where they take inspiration from — whether from historical events explicitly mentioned, broader cultural history, personal biography, mythology, literature, or political movements. For each song, identify the key lyrical references, explain the real-world context behind them, and show how the songwriter translated that source material into music. Examples:
- "The Wall" (Pink Floyd) — Roger Waters' personal experiences with isolation and his father's death in WWII
- "Masters of War" (Bob Dylan) — Cold War arms race and Eisenhower's farewell address
- "Born in the USA" (Springsteen) — Vietnam veterans' alienation, frequently misread as patriotic

Consider a similarity rating for how directly the lyrics map to the historical source (e.g. literal reference / inspired by / metaphorical echo).

Could also include:
- A "listen for" cue pointing to a specific line or verse
- A "historical context" block explaining the event/period referenced
- Emotion and activity tags (same as Harmony category)
